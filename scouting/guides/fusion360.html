How to Install Autodesk Fusion 360, and Import/Export STL files

(This is for Windows and Mac, but not Linux)

In box 1, click Sign in
Enter the email: beastbot2855@gmail.com
Enter the password: ask someone else on the team, I don't want to post it here
In box 2, click REGISTER
In box 3, click DOWNLOAD
Save/Open the file, allow it to download, then sign in with the username & password above

How to Import an STL file

Click the 3x3 of square dots in the top left corner
Click the Upload button
Click Select Files or Drag and Drop your files onto the popup
(If you download files in a zipped folder, you must copy them out frist!)
Once you have chosen all your files, click Upload

How to Export an STL file

In the top left of the modeling area, make sure you are in MODEL mode
Drag your mouse over everything you want to export
Click MAKE on the toolbar at the top
Click 3D Print Under Output uncheck Send to 3D Print Utility if it is checked
Click OK
Choose where to export to and give it a name
Click Save
