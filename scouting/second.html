Our Mission

SECOND strives to provide entertaining automaton contests for all northern delinquents.

Our Vision

We hope to one day have all northern delinquents participate in our program, so that we can increase education on automaton contests for all future generations. We hope that these trained northern delinquents will one day show future delinquents how to make automaton contests, so that the future northern delinquent's children can also teach their children, and so on, so that SECOND will leave a legacy of many lifetimes.
